Apple Pancake

Description

This delicious recipe is perfect for morning breakfast. One recipe is never enough for the family.

The pancakes are always moist and nutritious. I recommend these pancakes to be eaten with hot cinnamon syrup!

Ingredients(4 servings)

  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded tart apple
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
